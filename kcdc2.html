MVC and Webforms: Armed and Ready

A new project has come your way and you are faced with a decision: ASP.NET MVC or Web Forms? In this session, You will learn the information needed to properly make a choice. We will discuss the benefits and drawbacks of each approach, what to consider, and the undisputable facts that go along with each. Whether you are an architect or a developer, you will leave armed and ready to make an informed decision.
